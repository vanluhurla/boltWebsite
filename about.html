Our Story

Craftsmanship
meets passion.

Bolt was created for people who live, breathe and enjoy music.

We believe a pedalboard should be more than just a practical piece of gear. It should feel personal, reliable and made with purpose. That is why every Bolt board is designed with the musician in mind, combining functionality, style and attention to detail to create a setup that works beautifully on stage, in the studio or at home.

Our aim is to craft personalised pedalboards that not only meet expectations, but exceed them. Each board is made to suit the needs of the person using it, helping them keep their gear organised, accessible and ready to play.

With a genuine love for music and a strong dedication to the craft, we care about creating products that feel considered from start to finish.

  • Every board is handcrafted start to finish.
  • We work closely with each musician to understand their signal chain and stage requirements.
  • Built in the UK, shipped to musicians across Europe.
