Our Story

Craftsmanship
meets passion.

We've been building top-notch pedalboards and wiring setups for musicians all over Europe for over a decade. Our dedication to the craft and love for music drives everything we do.

Every board that leaves our workshop is handcrafted from start to finish — no assembly lines, no shortcuts. We work closely with each musician to understand their signal chain, their stage, and their sound.
